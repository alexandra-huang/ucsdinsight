Mentorship Program

The mentor-mentee program is a great way for members to gain more insight about the field of optometry and optometry school preparation. Members will be matched up with officers or upperclassmen to be their mentor. Mentees will be able to develop a closer bond with their mentors. Mentors will be advisors for mentees and share their knowledge and experience regarding pre-requisite courses, internships, optometry school applications, and many more! Our sign up for the mentor-mentee program occurs every fall quarter for the school year. If you've missed the sign up window, please email us for more information.
